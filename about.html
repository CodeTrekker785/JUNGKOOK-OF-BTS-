Biography

Jeon Jungkook

Jeon Jungkook is a South Korean singer, dancer, and songwriter, best known as a member of the global sensation BTS. Known as the "Golden Maknae," he is admired for his exceptional talent across singing, dancing, and performance.

Debuting with BTS in 2013, Jungkook quickly gained worldwide recognition for his powerful vocals, stage presence, and dedication to his craft. Over the years, he has become one of the most influential artists in the global music industry.
