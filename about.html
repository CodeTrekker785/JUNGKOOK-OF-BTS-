The Main Pop Boy

Jeon Jungkook is a South Korean singer and songwriter, globally recognized as the main vocalist of the 21st-century pop icons, BTS. Known as the "Golden Maknae," he possesses a rare combination of soulful vocals, sharp choreography, and undeniable charisma.

In 2023, he officially launched his solo career with the record-breaking single "Seven," followed by his debut studio album, GOLDEN. His music blends R&B, Pop, and Funk, proving his versatility as a solo powerhouse on the global stage.
